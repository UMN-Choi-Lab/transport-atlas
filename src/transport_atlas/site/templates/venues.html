Venue statistics

Per-venue paper / author / collaboration / citation stats — the atlas analogue of Sun & Rahwan (2017) Table 2. Author counts use the deduped author_key (OpenAlex ID → ORCID → normalized name). Collaborations = Σ C(n_p, 2) over papers with ≥2 authors.
Click any column header to sort. Methodology / policy journals (TR-B, Transport Reviews, Transportation Science) tend to have fewer authors per paper; tech journals (IEEE T-ITS, TR-C, T-IV) tend to have more — the same split Sun & Rahwan identified in 2017.
